Location


Tucked away from the main road, Baan Laksasubha is conveniently located in the center of Hua Hin and minutes away from various outlets such as the night market, coffee shops and restaurants. There are several ways to get to Hua Hin. Scheduled flights and daily trains from Bangkok to Hua Hin are available. Air-conditioned buses leave the Southern Bus Terminal every 30 minutes. Driving by car from Bangkok takes about two and a half hours.
